# Vendor Market Detail User Guide

## Overview

The Vendor Market Detail page provides comprehensive planning and management tools for vendors participating in markets. This guide covers all 6 main sections of the interface.

## Accessing Market Details

1. **From My Markets Page**: Click on any market you've applied to or been approved for
2. **From Search Results**: Click "View Details" on any market you're interested in
3. **From Dashboard**: Quick access to your active and upcoming markets

## 1. Overview Tab

### Market Information Panel
- **Market Name & Promoter**: Basic market details and contact information
- **Dates & Times**: Schedule information with countdown to event
- **Location & Directions**: Address with integrated map (when available)
- **Application Status**: Current status (draft, submitted, approved, rejected)

### Quick Actions Panel
- **Apply Now**: Submit your vendor application (if not yet submitted)
- **Edit Application**: Modify your existing application
- **Track Market**: Add to your tracked markets list
- **Export Data**: Download market details and your planning data

### Status Indicators
- **Event Status**: Upcoming, In Progress, Completed
- **Application Status**: Not Applied, Pending Review, Approved, Rejected, Booked
- **Planning Progress**: Percentage complete of preparation tasks

### Recent Activity Feed
- **Your Actions**: Recent todos completed, expenses added
- **Promoter Updates**: Important announcements from market organizers
- **Community Activity**: Recent comments and photos from other vendors

## 2. Preparation Tab

### Todo Management Interface

#### Todo Categories
- **Setup Tasks**: Booth assembly, display arrangement, signage
- **Inventory Tasks**: Product preparation, packaging, labeling
- **Marketing Tasks**: Social media posts, business cards, flyers
- **Logistics Tasks**: Transportation, parking, loading/unloading
- **Safety Tasks**: Emergency preparedness, insurance verification

#### Todo Templates
- **Farmers Market**: Pre-made todos for produce vendors
- **Arts & Crafts**: Templates for artist vendors
- **Food Vendors**: Specialized checklists for food handling
- **Custom Template**: Create your own reusable templates

#### Todo Features
- **Priority Levels**: Urgent, High, Medium, Low
- **Due Dates**: Set specific deadlines for tasks
- **Estimated Hours**: Track time commitment
- **Cost Estimates**: Associate costs with preparation tasks
- **Subtasks**: Break down complex tasks into smaller steps

#### Progress Tracking
- **Completion Percentage**: Visual progress indicators
- **Time Remaining**: Countdown to due dates
- **Cost Summary**: Total estimated vs actual costs
- **Productivity Insights**: Tasks completed per day/week

### Todo Actions
- **Mark Complete**: Check off finished tasks
- **Add Notes**: Document completion details or issues
- **Reassign Dates**: Adjust deadlines as needed
- **Duplicate Tasks**: Copy tasks between markets
- **Export Checklist**: Print or download todo lists

### Planning Tips
- **Start Early**: Begin planning 2-4 weeks before the market
- **Use Templates**: Save time with pre-made checklists
- **Daily Check-ins**: Review and update progress regularly
- **Be Flexible**: Adjust plans based on weather and other factors

## 3. Expenses Tab

### Budget Overview Dashboard

#### Summary Cards
- **Total Estimated Budget**: Sum of all planned expenses
- **Total Actual Expenses**: Money spent to date
- **Remaining Budget**: Difference between estimated and actual
- **Projected Profit**: Revenue minus total expenses

#### Expense Categories
- **Booth Fees**: Market participation costs
- **Transportation**: Fuel, vehicle maintenance, parking
- **Supplies & Equipment**: Display materials, tools, signage
- **Marketing & Promotion**: Business cards, flyers, online ads
- **Food & Meals**: Personal sustenance during market days
- **Insurance**: Coverage for liability and property
- **Miscellaneous**: Unexpected or one-time costs

### Expense Tracking Interface

#### Adding Expenses
- **Quick Add**: Enter basic expense information
- **Detailed Entry**: Include receipts, notes, categories
- **Bulk Import**: Upload expense data from spreadsheets
- **Photo Receipts**: Attach images of receipts for verification

#### Expense Fields
- **Title**: Descriptive name of the expense
- **Category**: Classification for reporting
- **Amount**: Cost in your preferred currency
- **Date**: When the expense was incurred
- **Payment Method**: Cash, card, check, etc.
- **Tax Deductible**: Mark for tax purposes
- **Notes**: Additional details or context

#### Budget vs Actual Comparison
- **Visual Charts**: Line graphs showing planned vs actual spending
- **Variance Reports**: Identify overspending areas
- **Trend Analysis**: Spending patterns over time
- **Forecasting**: Predict future expense needs

### Revenue Tracking

#### Sales Categories
- **Product Sales**: Direct merchandise revenue
- **Services**: Consultations, demonstrations, workshops
- **Commissions**: Percentage-based earnings
- **Tips/Donations**: Additional income sources

#### Revenue Analytics
- **Daily Totals**: Sales breakdown by market day
- **Product Performance**: Which items sell best
- **Customer Insights**: Popular price points and preferences
- **Profit Margins**: Revenue minus associated costs

### Budget Management Tips

#### Pre-Market Planning
- **Research Costs**: Ask other vendors about typical expenses
- **Build Buffer Funds**: Add 20-30% contingency for unexpected costs
- **Track Averages**: Learn from past market performance

#### During Market Management
- **Daily Expense Logs**: Record costs as they occur
- **Cash Flow Monitoring**: Track money coming in vs going out
- **Adjust Spending**: Cut back if sales are below expectations

#### Post-Market Analysis
- **Calculate ROI**: Compare profits to time and money invested
- **Identify Savings**: Learn from inefficient spending
- **Update Templates**: Improve future market budgeting

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### Application Problems
- **Submission Errors**: Check required fields and file formats
- **Payment Issues**: Verify billing information and retry transactions
- **Document Rejections**: Ensure all documents meet size/format requirements

#### Planning Challenges
- **Overwhelming Todo Lists**: Use templates and break tasks into smaller steps
- **Cost Overruns**: Set spending alerts and review budget regularly
- **Time Conflicts**: Prioritize urgent tasks and delegate when possible

#### Communication Issues
- **Delayed Responses**: Use multiple contact methods if urgent
- **Missing Messages**: Check spam folders and message archives
- **Technical Difficulties**: Use phone contact for critical communications

#### Technical Problems
- **App Crashes**: Clear cache, update app, restart device
- **Data Sync Issues**: Force refresh or log out/in to resync
- **File Upload Failures**: Check internet connection and file sizes
