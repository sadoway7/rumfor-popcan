# Community Photos

**Goal:** Encourage more photo uploads and engagement.

**Focus areas:**
- Mobile upload UX
- Compression + preview
- Clear hero-photo selection rules
- Photo permissions by role (vendor/promoter/community)
- Consent + reporting flow for inappropriate uploads

**Success metric:** Photo uploads per market up 30%.

**Success states:**
- Upload progress with cancel
- Hero selection shows “Selected by community” label

**Error states:**
- Upload failure shows retry + offline queue suggestion
- Moderation block shows reason + appeal link
